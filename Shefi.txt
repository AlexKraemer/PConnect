**Union Burst: Schneeich Tanz**

Shefi deals 249468 physical damage to the nearest enemy and applies 7 Kennzeichen stacks to herself.
Each time she uses Union Burst, the number of Kennzeichen stacks applied increases by 1. Kennzeichen stacks up to 20.
If Seegefroren's Field is being deployed, Shefi applies to herself a TP Regen buff that recovers 10 TP per second. The amount of TP recovered by this Skill increases with the number of Kennzeichen stacks.


**Skill 1: Viel Glück**

**Skill 2: Seegefroren**
