**Creditta (3★)**
Details:
Arena: ATK - C, DEF - B
CB: ST - A, Multi - SS
Quest: SS
Overall: S


**Union Burst: Million Flipping**
Creditta deals 43377 physical damage to all enemies in front of her and lowers their P.DEF by 74. The damage and P.DEF debuff potency increases with the number of enemies within the area of effect.


**Skill 1: Trap Wallet**
no UE: Creditta deals 41359 physical damage to all enemies in an area around the nearest enemy. The first usage of this Skill also lowers the P.DEF of enemies hit by 74.
w/ UE: Creditta deals 49871 physical damage to all enemies in an area around the nearest enemy. The first usage of this Skill also lowers the P.DEF of enemies hit by 74 and applies to them a debuff that increases the damage taken from Critical attacks by 2%.


**Skill 2: Royalty Right**
Creditta applies a Regen buff that heals for 1000 HP per second and a TP Regen buff that recovers 10 TP per second to all allies, and also deploys a Barrier that negates 3228 physical damage on all allies.


**EX Skill: Hesokuri Boost**
Creditta's P.ATK goes up by 4035 (gains an additional +225 P.ATK at ★5) at the start of the battle.
